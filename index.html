Annual International Conference of Faculty of Management Sciences Federal University of Lafia

Theme: Resilient Resource Management Amidst Economic Turmoil: Strategies for Sustainable National Development

Background

In times of economic turmoil, effective resource management is crucial for sustainable national development. This theme explores challenges posed by economic instability, emphasizing the need for resilient strategies. It aims to identify pathways for nations to withstand crises and maintain long-term progress. By understanding the link between instability and resource management, the goal is to provide policymakers, businesses, and communities with insights and tools to navigate economic challenges and foster sustainable development.

  • Dates: August 26 - 29, 2024
  • Venue: MIS Hall, Federal University of Lafia, Nasarawa State, Nigeria
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM Daily
  • Submission of Abstracts: May 10 - August 18, 2024
  • Submission of Full Papers: July 10 - August 25, 2024
